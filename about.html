EYEConic — Annual EyecareFest 2026

EYEConic 2026 is the Annual EyecareFest organized by Sunetra Family Eye Care Centre, in association with Dr Anil Biswas Community Eye Clinic.

Designed to bring together eye care professionals, practitioners, and innovators from Eastern India and beyond, EYEConic offers unmatched opportunities for scientific learning, clinical collaboration, and meaningful knowledge exchange.

The programme features expert national faculty, didactic sessions, workshops, oral presentations, and engaging academic activities — all promoting evidence-based discussion and skill development in ophthalmology and eye care.
