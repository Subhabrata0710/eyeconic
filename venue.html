Fairfield by Marriott, New Town, Kolkata

Fairfield by Marriott, New Town

Located in New Town — Kolkata's vibrant emerging business hub — Ozone Convention Centre, Fairfield by Marriott offers a modern, premium setting perfectly suited for EYEConic 2026. The venue features world-class conference facilities, excellent dining, and is well-connected across the city.

📍 Address: Ozone Convention Centre, Fairfield by Marriott, New Town, Kolkata
✈️ Airport: NSCBI Airport — ~30 min
🚗 City Centre: Well connected via major roads and app cabs
🚇 Nearest Metro: New Town (Orange Line / Extended Metro)

How to Reach

✈️

By Air

Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport is approximately 30 km away. Pre-paid taxis and app cabs (Ola/Uber) are readily available.

🚂

By Train

Sealdah Station: ~12 km
Howrah Station: ~16 km
Both stations are well connected via road and cabs to New Town.

🚇

By Metro & Road

New Town is accessible via the extended metro line and well-connected arterial roads. App cabs, auto-rickshaws, and buses ply regularly.
