Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is EYEConic 2026?+

EYEConic 2026 will be held on Sunday, 26 July 2026 at Ozone Convention Centre, Fairfield by Marriott, New Town, Kolkata. It is well connected by road and approximately 30 minutes from the airport.

How do I register?+

Simply fill out the registration form on our Register page and submit your details. You will receive a confirmation email with your Registration ID and QR Code.

Who can attend EYEConic 2026?+

EYEConic 2026 is open to ophthalmologists, optometrists, residents and trainees, allied eye care professionals, and faculty. All eye care practitioners are welcome.

Will certificates be provided?+

Yes, all registered delegates will receive a participation certificate. CME credits may also be awarded for attending the scientific sessions.

Can I submit an abstract?+

Yes! After registration, login to your dashboard to upload abstracts for oral presentations. Contact us at eyeconicbysunetra@gmail.com for submission guidelines.
