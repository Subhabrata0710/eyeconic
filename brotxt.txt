 About
 EZECON & Bengal EM Update 2026
 EZECON 2026 (East Zone Emergency Medicine Conference &
 Bengal EM Update) is the flagship annual academic conference of
 SEMI West Bengal Chapter, designed to bring together emergency
 and critical care professionals from across Eastern India and
 beyond on a single focused platform for learning, collaboration, and
 exchange of ideas.
 EZECON 2025 recorded more than 450 delegate registrations, with
 faculty participation from across India and international faculty from
 the United Kingdom strengthening the academic momentum for
 EZECON 2026.
 The conference will feature a balanced and practice-oriented
 scientific programme with national faculty, didactic sessions, pre-
 conference workshops, oral presentations, quiz, and WBMC credit
 points.
 The programme is intended to promote evidence-based
 discussion, hands-on skill development, and meaningful academic
 engagement in emergency medicine and acute care.

 ORAL PRESENTATION
 Guidelines
 EZECON 2026 invites abstract submissions for oral presentations from trainees, residents, MOS
 and registrars in Emergency Medicine, Critical Care, and allied acute care specialties.
 Accepted Categories
 Oral presentations may be submitted under the following broad themes:
 Emergency Medicine & Critical Care | Trauma & Surgical Emergencies | Paediatric
 Emergencies |
 Internal Medicine & Allied Specialty Emergencies | Interesting Case Reports/
 Case Series
 Abstract Submission
 Abstracts must be submitted directly through the conference website: www.ezeconsemi.com
 Abstract submission deadline: 26 July 2026, 11:59 PM IST
 Selected presenters will be informed by email regarding acceptance/rejection and oral
 presentation date/time.
 Presentation Format
 Selected abstracts will be presented as oral slide presentations during EZECON 2026.
 Presentation duration: 8 minutes presentation + 2 minutes discussion.
 Selection, Certificates & Awards
 All submitted abstracts will undergo review by the Scientific Committee. Selected oral
 presentations will receive certificates of presentation.
 The Top 3 Oral Presentation Awards will
 be judged by an impartial neutral team of judges and recognised during EZECON 2026.
 Full Guideline
 For detailed submission guidelines, eligibility criteria, abstract structure, declaration
 requirements, presentation instructions, consent requirements, selection and award criteria,
 please visit: www.ezeconsemi.com
