Abstract Submission — EYEConic 2026

EYEConic 2026 invites abstract submissions for Oral Presentations from ophthalmologists, optometrists, residents, and allied eye care professionals.

The Oral Presentation session is designed to provide a scientific platform for presenting original research, case reports, case series, and clinically relevant academic work related to ophthalmology and eye care.

All submitted abstracts will undergo review by the Scientific Committee. Selected abstracts will be presented during EYEConic 2026.
