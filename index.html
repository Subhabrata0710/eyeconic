Welcome to EYEConic 2026

A premier eyecare summit bringing together leading experts in Ophthalmology & Eye Care

We are delighted to invite you to EYEConic 2026 — the Annual EyecareFest to be held on Sunday, 26th July 2026 at Ozone Convention Centre, Fairfield by Marriott, New Town, Kolkata.

Organized by Sunetra Family Eye Care Centre, Somany Surgical Eye Clinic & Dr Anil Biswas Community Eye Clinic, EYEConic 2026 brings together leading ophthalmologists, eye care professionals, and practitioners for a day of focused scientific exchange and innovation.

The conference theme — "Where Vision Meets Commitment" — reflects our dedication to advancing eye care excellence through science, collaboration, and unwavering commitment to patient vision.
